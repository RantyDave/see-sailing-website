Privacy Policy

By uploading data to See Sailing you are granting us a license to use that data for the purposes of further analysis and display. Please note that this data by necessity includes the time and location of your boat.
We will not share your data with third parties without your consent.

We will contact you via the email address associated with your credit card with information necessary to the operation of your See Sailing account.

We do not hold any credit card or similar payment details, all payments are handled by Stripe Inc.
