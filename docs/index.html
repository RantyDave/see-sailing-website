Creating an account

See Sailing is subscription software, paid for on an annual basis but with a thirty day trial period. You will need to provide valid credit card details to create an account but won't be charged if you cancel within thirty days. There is a single 'tier' subscription that applies for a single boat with no additional usage charges; and the mobile app is provided free of charge.

Subscriptions are managed by Stripe and you start a new one by clicking here...

SUBSCRIBE

When you've done that an email will be sent with your login details and a link to your account page (something like https://app.see-sailing.com/u/yourname). You can use this page to change your username, password and the name of your boat and this is described in detail on the management page documentation.
