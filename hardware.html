The Easy Way

No messing around with memory cards. No need to attach a laptop.
No wifi, passwords, or network configuration.

A single button uploads to the Internet.
Your analysis will be online within a few minutes.

The gateway connects your yacht to your phone with Bluetooth.
See the status and activity of your NMEA 2000 network at a glance.

Installation is by slotting into your existing NMEA backbone.
Simply scan the QR code on the front of the gateway to be taken to
a setup page and downloads for the app.

Power consumption is 35ma (1 LEN), device is 52mm x 43mm x 26mm.
No moving parts (electronics are buried in resin).

More details in the documentation.
